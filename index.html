Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QuizNest Brain.

What are the subscription charges?

The subscription fee is (2.78 BDT / 5.56 BDT) including (Vat+SC+SD). This service is exclusively available for Robi and Airtel Users in Bangladesh.

What platforms are supported?

Currently, QuizNest Brain is exclusively available for Android devices running Android 8.0 or newer. Ensure you enable "Install from Unknown Sources" when installing the APK.

How do I unsubscribe?

You can easily unsubscribe at any time by opening the app, navigating to the Profile screen, and tapping the red "Unsubscribe & Log Out" button. You will be instantly unsubscribed and logged out.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We do not collect or store passwords, emails, or personal identification. We only use your mobile number for secure BDApps authentication and subscription management.
